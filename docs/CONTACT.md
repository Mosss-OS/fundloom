# Contact Information

Last updated: April 2026

## General Inquiries

For general questions about Fundloom, please contact us at:
**info@fundloom.io**

We typically respond to general inquiries within 1-2 business days.

## Support

If you need help using the platform or have encountered an issue, please contact our support team at:
**support@fundloom.io**

Our support team is available to assist with:
- Account access and wallet connection issues
- Transaction problems
- Campaign creation and management questions
- Technical difficulties with the platform
- General usage guidance

We aim to respond to support tickets within 4-8 hours during business hours (9 AM - 5 PM UTC).
